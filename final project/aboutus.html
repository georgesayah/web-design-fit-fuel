About FitFuel Sports

FitFuel Sports was founded in 2018 in the heart of Zahlé, Lebanon, by two lifelong friends, Georges Sayah and Anthony Haddad.

What started as a small passion project quickly grew into one of the region’s most trusted sports and fitness retailers. Our mission has always been simple: to bring premium-quality sportswear, footwear, and accessories to athletes of all levels — whether you’re stepping into the gym for the first time or training to compete professionally.

Today, FitFuel Sports proudly collaborates with global brands, supports local athletes, and continues to expand its online and in-store presence. From basketball to football to gym essentials, we are committed to fueling your journey with the gear you deserve.
